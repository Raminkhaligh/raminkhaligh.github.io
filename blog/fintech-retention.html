How I Increased User Retention by 40%

Industry: Fintech • Role: Product & Growth Lead • Duration: 12 weeks

The Challenge

The product had great acquisition but struggled with churn. Only 32% of users returned after 30 days. My goal was to identify why and design data-informed solutions to drive retention above 50%.

What I Did

  • Conducted user interviews to identify drop-off moments and unfulfilled value promises.
  • Mapped the entire activation funnel and defined key engagement milestones.
  • Launched three A/B tests targeting onboarding, notifications, and feature discoverability.
  • Introduced lifecycle automation (email + push) tied to user behavior.

Results

Within 12 weeks, active user retention increased by +40%. The onboarding completion rate grew from 48% to 71%, and overall NPS improved by 12 points.

“Ramin turned data into action. His approach transformed our retention curve.” — Growth Director, Wallex

Takeaways

  • Retention begins at activation — clarity beats complexity.
  • Cross-team alignment on KPIs is key to sustained growth.
  • Micro-feedback loops can guide product iteration faster than large surveys.
