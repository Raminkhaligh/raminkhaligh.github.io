About Me

With 8+ years at the intersection of product, growth and UX, I help SaaS and fintech teams and beyond ship results. My approach blends research, analytics and creativity — crafting products people keep using.

I’m passionate about bridging the gap between product and marketing teams and translating user insights into actionable strategies. My multicultural perspective (fluent in English and Persian) allows me to collaborate across diverse teams.

Over the years I’ve delivered measurable impact: at Wallex I built a 15‑person MarTech squad that drove six‑figure revenue growth and lifted organic traffic by +75%; at Tabdeal I launched a portfolio recommendation engine that attracted $1.5M+ in user inflow. These achievements fuel my belief that thoughtful product and growth work can materially move the needle for startups.
